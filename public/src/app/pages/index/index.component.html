How it Works

  1. Fill in your name and email address.
  2. Select your skill level.
  3. Select your desired workout type (optional).
  4. Select the muscle group you want to focus on (optional).
  5. Select your desired rep scheme (optional).
  6. Select how long you want your workout to last.
  7. Enter you PRs (personal records) on the four exercises listed. NOTE: if you don't know what your PR is enter "0".
  8. Select all the equipment you have available, and want to use during your workout.
  9. Click or tap "Give me my custom workout" and off you go!
  10. Don't forget to give it 110%!
